About Us

ClearUFO.Space is dedicated to uncovering the truth hidden within an image or video footage. I am originally an independent researcher and have personally learned how to navigate my way around using video forensics software to investigate my own personal footage. Upon achieving positive feedback from world renowned crime scene analysts in the field, I decided to provide services in this specific area of interest.

With extreme attention to detail that most people miss, I have assisted investigators in solving complex cases by enhancing the visibility of crucial information from various forms of video evidence, particularly those types of videos containing cloaked beings as in the May 1st, 2023 Las Vegas Incident.

My commitment to accuracy, confidentiality, and innovation sets myself apart in the world of video forensics. I am continuously improving in developing my in house technique and methodologies to ensure I always deliver the most reliable and comprehensive analysis possible.
